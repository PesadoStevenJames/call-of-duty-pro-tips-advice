Loadout Suggestions

The KRM-262 with this setup is perfect for close-range dominance. The Marauder Suppressor and Extended Barrel boost range for consistent one-shots, while No Stock and the MIP Laser 5mW give faster ADS and hip-fire accuracy. Paired with Speed Up Kill, it’s built for aggressive rushing and chaining kills with ease.

The BP50 build is perfect for aggressive play. The Maxim Silencer keeps you stealthy, the Rapid Barrel and Custom Stock boost mobility and accuracy, while the 80 Round Drums let you mow down enemies without frequent reloads. Paired with the Granular Grip, it’s a fast, stable, and deadly setup.
